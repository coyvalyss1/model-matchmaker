# Art Ping Pong - Reddit & Social Posts

## r/Parenting Post (Story-first)

**Title:**
My family started taking turns making AI art and it became our favorite screen time

**Body:**
We have a rule in our house: no mindless scrolling. But enforcing that with a 10-year-old and a 13-year-old is exhausting.

Then a few months ago we started doing this thing: one person makes an art piece with AI, the next person builds on it, back and forth. We call it art ping pong.

At first it was just something to try. But it turned into the thing my kids actually ask to do. Not because I make them. Because it's fun.

My daughter made a sunset over mountains. My son added a dragon. I added a castle. My daughter added villagers. Back and forth. By the end we had this whole world we built together.

Now we print the good ones and hang them in the hallway. Guests ask "who made that?" and my kids say "we all did."

We turned it into an app so other families could do it. It's called Art Ping Pong. There's an AI art teacher named Ross who guides you through it (doesn't do the work for you, just teaches you how to make better art).
